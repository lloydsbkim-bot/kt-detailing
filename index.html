Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

Draft answers below — edit these to match exactly how you work.

How long does a detail take?

An exterior detail typically takes about 1 to 2 hours. An interior detail can take 2 to 5 hours depending on the condition and size of the vehicle. Extremely dirty vehicles or trucks may incur an additional $25–$100 charge.

Do I need to provide water or power?

Yes — a garden hose is needed for exterior cleaning, and access to power from your home will likely be required as well.

What forms of payment do you accept?

Cash is preferred, though e-transfer is also accepted.

What happens if it rains on my scheduled day?

If it rains, we'll either reschedule or complete an interior-only detail, though results may not be as thorough as usual in that case.

How far in advance should I book?

Booking at least 1 to 2 days in advance is recommended, though we'll do our best to accommodate shorter notice when possible.
